The mission of ProjectSpeech is to foster growth of CS (Computer Science) to everyone thorugh voice-activated programming. This way, those with disabilities or those not as fortunate have equal opportunities as others in the STEM field. ProjectSpeech fosters growth, creavitivity, and innovation and supports other organizations to help spread the values of ProjectSpeech. Through promoting outreach programs such as robotics clubs and summer programs, ProjectSpeech hopes to greatly expand the STEM field with new innovative minds that will shape the future.
